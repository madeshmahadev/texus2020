TEXUS 2019

Texus 2019 was a collaboration born out of imagination, rooted in pragmatic realization and inspired by an indomitable, undaunted aura of inspiration. Texus 2019 raised its bar in both the aspects of the intellectual as well as cultural size from organizing Rush Hackathon, sponsored by Github along with other technical events and workshops. This time, we also had a Walkathon for creating public awareness and to sustain greenery. Texus 2019 took rest with the dazzling DJ night by DJ Ravator and International DJ Sabrina.
