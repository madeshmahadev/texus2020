ABOUT TEXUS 2020

Texus 2020 is a conglomeration of ideas, of fervor of passion, of conceptions of convictions and a fertile ground for the birth of undaunted perceptions. It is a National Level Tech Fest organized by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, SRM IST, Ramapuram. This extravaganza of mirth requires a point of reference, a one stop family shop that exhibits the mele of wonders the this fest beholds.

Due to Corona virus outbreak worldwide and UGC (University Grants Commission of India) norms, the authorities have decided to postpone Texus 2020. Stay Tuned We Will Be Back Soon!!
